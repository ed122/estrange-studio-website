Studio

A visual practice shaped by architecture, time and small shifts in light.

Vision

Estrange Studio crafts imagery and narratives that sit between the built and the imagined. Each project is treated as a quiet study in space, atmosphere and human scale — whether it lives as a single frame or a longer moving story.

Our work is collaborative by design: we listen, edit and refine until the visuals feel inevitable, not decorative. The aim is simple — to make architecture felt before it is built.

Practice

Approach

We move from broad gesture to detail: from early tonal sketches and light studies through to calibrated materials, people and atmosphere. Every commission is built around a shared storyboard and a clear production rhythm.

What we enjoy

  • Competition and concept imagery
  • Atmospheric narratives for film and animation
  • Long-term visual identities for studios and brands
